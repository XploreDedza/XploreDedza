Top Destinations

Must-visit places in and around Dedza

Dedza Mountain

The highest point in central Malawi offering panoramic views, hiking trails, and unique rock formations.

Difficulty: Moderate

Duration: 4-6 hours

Dedza Pottery

Malawi's oldest pottery workshop where you can watch artisans create beautiful ceramics and even try your hand at pottery.

Shop & Cafe Onsite

Open Daily 8AM-5PM

Chongoni Rock Art Area

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring ancient rock paintings that date back over 2,000 years.

UNESCO Site

Ancient History

Dedza Forest Reserve

A protected area with diverse plant and animal species, perfect for bird watching and nature walks.

Pine Forest

Bird Watching

Ntchisi Forest Lodge

A beautiful eco-lodge located in a rainforest reserve, offering hiking and cultural experiences.

Eco-Lodge

Local Cuisine

Dedza Town

The charming town center with local markets, craft shops, and a glimpse into everyday Malawian life.

Local Crafts

Street Food
