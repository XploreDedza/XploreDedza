About Dedza

Dedza District is a vibrant landscape of rolling mountains, traditional pottery, rock art and warm communities. XploreDedza celebrates local culture and eco-tourism while supporting conservation and sustainable livelihoods.

  • Gule Wamkulu cultural heritage
  • Renowned Dedza pottery and artisans
  • Trails, peaks and panoramic views
