About Dedza

The Heart of Malawi

Dedza is a picturesque district in the Central Region of Malawi, known for its stunning highland scenery, rich cultural heritage, and welcoming communities. Situated at an altitude of around 1,600 meters, Dedza offers a refreshing climate and breathtaking views that captivate every visitor.

Our mission at XploreDedza is to showcase the beauty and diversity of this remarkable region. From the majestic Dedza Mountain to the ancient rock art sites, from thriving pottery industries to lush pine forests, Dedza has something for every traveler.

Top Attractions

Dedza Mountain

At 2,198 meters, Dedza Mountain is the highest point in the Central Region. Hike through montane forests to reach the summit for panoramic views stretching as far as Lake Malawi.

Learn More →

Ancient Rock Art

Discover UNESCO-listed rock art sites dating back over 2,000 years. These remarkable paintings provide a window into the spiritual lives of the ancient Chewa people.

Learn More →

Dedza Pottery

Visit Malawi's oldest pottery workshop, established in 1956. Watch skilled artisans create beautiful ceramics and try your hand at the potter's wheel in a hands-on workshop.

Popular Activities

Mountain Trekking

Explore diverse trails through pine forests, grasslands, and rocky outcrops with experienced local guides.

Culinary Tours

Taste traditional Malawian dishes, visit local markets, and learn cooking techniques from village chefs.

Bird Watching

Spot over 200 bird species in Dedza's diverse habitats, including rare montane specialists.

Cultural Experiences

Engage with local communities, learn traditional dances, and participate in craft workshops.
