About MUREF

Founded by Hon. Vitumbiko Mumba, MUREF is a community-based microloan initiative focused on sustainable development in Mzimba Central. It provides group-based loans to small-scale entrepreneurs, youth, and women.

Philosophy

“Let’s stop celebrating poverty by handing out coffins.”

— Hon. Mumba (English)

“Tisiyeni kutumbika umphawi ndi kupereka makofi.”

— Hon. Mumba (Tumbuka)
